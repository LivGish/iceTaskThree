My favourite movie: To All the Boys I've Loved Before


Lara Jean, a high school girl, writes letters to the boys she is infatuated with but keeps them in her closet instead of mailing them. Things change when her sister discovers the letters.


Movie cast:

  • Lana Condor as Lara Jean
  • Noah Centineo as Peter
  • Anna Cathcart as Kitty
  • Israel Broussard as Josh
  • Janel Parrish as Margot
  • Emilija Baranac as Gen
  • John Corbett as Dr. Covey
